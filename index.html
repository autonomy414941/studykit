Study Planning FAQ

What GPA scale does this use?
The GPA and Target GPA tools support common 4.0, 4.3, and 5.0 scale assumptions.

What if my instructor has a different grading model?
Use your official course rubric as the final source. StudyKit is for quick planning estimates.

How should I use this with a packed schedule?
Pair your study estimate with FocusKit to test if your week has enough capacity.
