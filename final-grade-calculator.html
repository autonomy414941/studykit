Final Grade FAQ

How is the required final score calculated?
Required score = (target - current x completed weight) / final exam weight.

What if my final is not the only remaining assessment?
Adjust the completed-weight input so the remaining portion matches your actual syllabus.

What should I do if I need a very high score?
Prioritize this course in your weekly plan using the Study Time Calculator.
