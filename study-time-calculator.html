Study Time FAQ

How conservative should base hours be?
Use your recent real pace, then add weak-topic overhead instead of forcing an aggressive baseline.

Why include rest days?
Scheduled recovery reduces burnout and preserves quality in the final week.

What if this plan looks too heavy?
Lower the topic scope, extend timeline, or prioritize highest-weight exam sections first.
